The firm

My commitment

I began my career in Barcelona, where I practiced as a lawyer for several years, working primarily in support of migrant workers, unaccompanied minors, and other migrants in highly vulnerable situations.

In 2022, I moved to France to pursue a Master's degree in Migrations with a specialization in International and European Law. During this time, I continued volunteering with NGOs such as La Cimade and Amnesty International.

These experiences have shaped my way of practicing law: with thoroughness, empathy, and humanity.
